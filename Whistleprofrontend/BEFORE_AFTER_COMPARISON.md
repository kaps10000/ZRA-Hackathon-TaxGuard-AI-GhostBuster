## 🎨 Color Psychology & Usage

### ZRA Green (#006633)
- **Meaning**: Growth, prosperity, trust, nature
- **Usage**: Primary buttons, headers, badges, icons
- **Zambian Context**: From national flag, represents natural resources

### ZRA Red (#DE2010)
- **Meaning**: Struggle, freedom, importance
- **Usage**: Alerts, error messages, critical actions
- **Zambian Context**: From national flag, represents struggle for freedom

### Copper Orange (#EF7D00)
- **Meaning**: Warmth, copper heritage, energy
- **Usage**: Warnings, accent elements, highlights
- **Zambian Context**: Zambia's copper mining industry

### Gold Yellow (#FFB81C)
- **Meaning**: Prosperity, wealth, mineral resources
- **Usage**: Success states, positive feedback
- **Zambian Context**: Mineral wealth and prosperity
