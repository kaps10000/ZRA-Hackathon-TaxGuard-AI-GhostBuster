🚀 Project Proposal: TaxGuard AI + GhostBuster

Tagline: “Smarter enforcement. Safer citizens. Stronger revenue.”

📌 Definition

TaxGuard AI is an intelligent compliance platform for ZRA that uses AI + Blockchain + Citizen Engagement to stop tax evasion, fraud, and corruption.

It has a unique GhostBuster module that detects fake companies and phantom employees by cross-checking payroll and taxpayer data against national databases.

🧩 Key Modules

AI Risk Scoring

ML assigns compliance risk scores to taxpayers.

ZRA can prioritize audits on top 10% riskiest cases.

GhostBuster 👻

Detects fake/phantom companies and ghost employees.

Cross-checks payroll vs NAPSA, NRC, PACRA, ECZ.

Flags shell companies with fake directors or shared TPIN contacts.

WhistlePro (Secure Reporting)

Anonymous, encrypted reporting portal.

Auto-scrubs metadata, gives citizens a case ID, and logs reports on blockchain.

Blockchain Ledger

Immutable audit trail of reports & enforcement actions.

Builds trust that no one can delete or alter cases.

Predictive Analytics

Forecasts tax revenue using external data (copper prices, GDP, imports).

Helps government prepare for shocks.

🎯 Impact

ZRA: Detect fraud faster, focus audits, recover lost revenue.

Citizens: Protected & empowered to report evasion.

Government: Data-driven policy & foresight.

⚙ Tech Stack

ML/AI: Python (Scikit-learn, XGBoost, LightGBM)

Data: Pandas/Spark, SQL, APIs (NAPSA, PACRA, NRC)

Blockchain: Hyperledger Fabric (permissioned)

Frontend: React + D3.js (dashboards & alerts)

Backend: Django/Flask with REST & GraphQL APIs

Security: AES-256 encryption, JWT tokens, Metadata scrubbing

📊 Hackathon Demo Flow

Upload taxpayer dataset → Dashboard shows risk scores & heatmap.

Submit anonymous whistleblower report → instantly logged on blockchain.

GhostBuster runs cross-check → flags phantom employees/ghost company.

Simulate copper price drop → predictive model forecasts tax impact.
